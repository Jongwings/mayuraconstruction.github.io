FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Mayura Construction offer?
Mayura Construction & Infrastructure offers a comprehensive range of services including Building Construction, Structural Consultancy, DTCP / LPA Approval, Interior Design & Execution, Renovation & Remodeling, Land Survey & Estimation, Building Stability Certification, and Turnkey Project Solutions.
How can I request a quote for my project?
Visit our Contact page, call us at +91 94878 62233, or send an email. Our team will schedule a site visit and provide a detailed, transparent quotation with no hidden costs.
Does Mayura Construction handle both small and large-scale projects?
Yes. We have the expertise, resources, and dedicated teams to handle projects of any scale — from single-room renovations to large multi-storey constructions and full infrastructure development.
What sets Mayura Construction apart?
Our edge lies in years of experience, superior craftsmanship, transparent communication, and a deeply client-focused approach. We deliver on time, within budget, and with a quality guarantee on all work.
Is Mayura Construction licensed and insured?
Yes. Mayura Construction & Infrastructure is fully licensed and insured, adhering to all regulatory requirements to give clients complete confidence and peace of mind throughout every project.
How long does a typical construction project take?
Duration varies by scope and complexity. Small renovations may take weeks, while large-scale projects can span months to years. We always provide a clear timeline upfront and keep you updated throughout.
Does Mayura Construction provide a warranty on its work?
Yes. We provide a warranty on all completed work to ensure customer satisfaction and long-term confidence. Warranty specifics are agreed upon at the contract stage and vary by project type.
Does Mayura Construction also provide warranty?
Yes. Mayura Construction & Infrastructure provides warranties on all work undertaken, covering quality assurance and customer satisfaction. Duration and coverage details are specified in the project agreement.
How much do Mayura Construction projects cost?
Project cost depends on scope, size, complexity, materials used, labour, and location. After an initial consultation and site visit, we provide a detailed, itemised estimate — no surprises, maximum value.
