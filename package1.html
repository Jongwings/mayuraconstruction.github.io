Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customise a package to suit my requirements?
Absolutely. All three packages serve as a starting point. We work with each client to tailor the scope, materials, and finishes to their exact needs and budget. Contact us for a no-obligation consultation.
What is included in the project price estimate?
The per sq.ft rate covers civil work, structural materials, labour, basic MEP (mechanical, electrical, and plumbing), and standard finishes as described in each package. External approvals, compound walls, and landscaping are quoted separately.
How long does a typical residential project take?
A standard 1,000 sq.ft residential build typically takes 8–12 months from groundbreaking to handover depending on the package, design complexity, and site conditions. We provide a detailed project timeline before work begins.
What payment schedule do you follow?
We follow a milestone-based payment structure — typically split across 5–6 stages from foundation to handover. This ensures transparency and aligns payments with physical progress on site.
Do you handle building plan approvals and permits?
Yes. Our team assists with plan drawing, municipality approvals, and all required construction permits. We guide you through the entire regulatory process so you can focus on your project.
